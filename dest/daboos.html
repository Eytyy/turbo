Translating to Needle in English, Turbo was approached to come up with the visual identity for Daboos; an embroidery art start-up and creative studio. The concept draws inspiration from old comic books.
The illustration for the logo was developed with esteemed illustrator Hassan Manasrah.
Daboos and Turbo are currently collaborating to produce a series of limited edition embroidered patches and a clothing line
