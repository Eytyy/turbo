Diversity or Death by Melika Quteishat and Soraya Ghezelbash.
The work investigates the botanical heritage of the Mediterranean, and more specifically the flora currently found within the urban landscape of Amman. Diversity or Death looks into the moral limits and responsibilities of human intervention on nature. Is the question of preservation independent of time and geographical designation? And to what extent should humans try to control nature's transformation?
