Never Records is a combination recording studio and record shop, all operating in one building. The sole proprietor, interior decorator, and engineer is New York-based artist Ted Riederer. Riederer devised his unique community art installation in the early part of this century and has been replicating it with regional variations throughout the world since 2010.
Performers and musician can sign up for recording sessions inside the art installation or ahead of time, with no auditions necessary, and leave with a freshly cut vinyl record of their music. Visitors to the exhibition can view all of the unique artwork in the installation, listen to any of the recordings from all of the previous Never Records on a turntable in the shop, and watch performances being recorded and cut live.
For the Amman installment of Never Records, Turbo developed the super-graphic and poster artwork.
